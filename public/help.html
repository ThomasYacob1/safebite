How to use SafeBite

Follow these steps to find recall information quickly and understand what the results mean.

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Search for a Product

Type a product name into the search bar on the Home page — for example "peanut butter", "spinach", or "chicken". Then click Search or press Enter.

2

Apply a Class Filter

Use the dropdown next to the search bar to filter by recall class. Choose Class I to see only the most dangerous recalls, or leave it on "All Classes" to see everything.

3

Review the Recall Cards

Each result card shows the product name, reason for recall, severity badge, and the date of the recall. Cards are sorted by most recent first.

4

Click a Card for Full Details

Click any recall card to open a detail panel showing the full product description, recalling firm, distribution area, quantity, and recall status.

5

Check the Distribution Area

Inside the detail panel, the Distribution Pattern field tells you which states or regions were affected. If your area is listed, take extra caution.

6

Use Recent Searches

Below the search bar, your past searches appear as quick-access pills. Click any pill to instantly re-run that search without typing.

7

Browse Multiple Pages

If your search returns many results, use the Prev and Next buttons at the bottom of the page to move between pages of 20 results.

Tip: Try searching broad terms like "dairy", "meat", or "flour" to see all recent recalls in a food category.
